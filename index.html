About CVIP 2026

The 11th International Conference on Computer Vision and Image Processing (CVIP 2026) is a premier global forum for researchers and practitioners. Organized jointly by NIT Calicut (India) and NTNU (Norway), the conference features high-quality research, keynote talks, and workshops.


The proceedings will be published in the Springer Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series (Scopus Indexed)[cite: 1].

Host Institute: NIT Calicut

National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) is one of the premier technical institutions in India. Set in a picturesque landscape at the foothills of the Western Ghats, NITC is a place of higher learning and research.


Conference Dates: December 9–12, 2026

Location: Kozhikode (Calicut), Kerala, India
